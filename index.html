About me!

Hello! I'm a transgender programmer girl who spends most of her time programming or playing games (unless I'm out with friends of course)!
I mainly program to help others, whether it's listing all pony conventions on a single website, improving event schedules for those in a MMORPG game, or building a replacement to a widely-used piece of software that really needs some improvement, all of it is to help others with their tasks (even this website is built with the intention of helping others get to know me and know how to contact me)!
I'm always willing to talk to others, unless I'm really busy, so don't be afraid to send a message or start a discussion with me!
